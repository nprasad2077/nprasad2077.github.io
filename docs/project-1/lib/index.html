How to Play:


  • All cards have a Pokémon on one side. Each Pokémon appears twice.
  • The game starts with all of the cards face down.
  • The player flips over two cards.
  • If the two cards are identical, the player scores a point and the cards remain facing up.
  • If the cards do not match, they will be turned face-down again.
  • The game is complete once all pairs of cards have been matched and all cards are facing up.
  • Gotta Match 'Em All! Good Luck. ☺️
